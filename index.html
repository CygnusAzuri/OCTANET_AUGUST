Aquatic Animals

Clownfish

  • Habitat: Coral reefs
  • Diet: Omnivores, feeding on algae and small invertebrates
  • Special Feature: Mutualistic relationship with sea anemones
  • Size: Up to 11 cm

Dolphin

  • Habitat: Marine environments, from shallow coastal waters to deep ocean
  • Diet: Carnivorous, primarily fish and squid
  • Special Feature: Highly intelligent and social animals
  • Size: 1.7 to 4 meters

Great White Shark

  • Habitat: Coastal waters in all major oceans
  • Diet: Carnivorous, mainly fish and marine mammals
  • Special Feature: Apex predator with keen sense of smell
  • Size: Up to 6 meters

Jellyfish

  • Habitat: Found in all ocean waters
  • Diet: Carnivorous, feeding on plankton, crustaceans, and small fish
  • Special Feature: Tentacles with stinging cells called nematocysts
  • Size: From a few millimeters to over 2 meters

Land Animals

Lion

  • Habitat: Grasslands and savannas
  • Diet: Carnivorous, primarily large herbivores
  • Special Feature: Social animals living in prides
  • Size: Up to 2.6 meters in length

Elephant

  • Habitat: Savannas, forests, and grasslands
  • Diet: Herbivorous, feeding on plants, fruits, and bark
  • Special Feature: Large ears and trunk
  • Size: Up to 4 meters tall

Giraffe

  • Habitat: Savannahs and open woodlands
  • Diet: Herbivorous, feeding mainly on leaves
  • Special Feature: Long neck and legs
  • Size: Up to 5.5 meters tall

Zebra

  • Habitat: Grasslands and savannas
  • Diet: Herbivorous, feeding on grass
  • Special Feature: Distinctive black and white stripes
  • Size: Up to 2.5 meters tall
